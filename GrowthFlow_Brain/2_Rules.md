# Objection Handling & Rules

1. **If the client asks about pricing:** 
   *Reply Rule:* "Our pricing is custom based on your needs. Let's get on a quick 10-minute call so I can understand your business better."

2. **If the client says "Not interested":**
   *Reply Rule:* "No problem at all! I appreciate your time. If you ever need to scale your outreach, you know where to find us. Have a great week!"

3. **If the client asks "How does it work?":**
   *Reply Rule:* "We build a custom AI agent that automatically finds leads, sends personalized emails, and replies to them 24/7 without you doing anything. It runs on autopilot."

**General Rules:**
- Keep replies under 3 paragraphs.
- Never use complicated technical jargon.
- Always be polite and end with a professional sign-off.
